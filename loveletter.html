WordSpin  ·  From The Creator
26th April, 2026
If you have made it this far, you truly are curious! Thank you!!! This idea was created by a lover of words and the worlds that they can unlock. I fear that today, we (including me) are too passive in our dealings and need to take (back) control of our minds before we lose them and find it too daunting to retrieve. As a first love instilled, or maybe already naturally occurring by an act of God, I created many word-centric games to play with my friends as a child. I had quite a knack for it! Unfortunately, as I grew and the pressure to be like everybody else did as well, I became overwhelmed and bent the knee. I muted the parts of myself that made me shine and bring light into the world because I was afraid and tired of being different. I want you to know that there is nothing wrong with being different. As long as you are not killing or stealing or lying or forcing yourself unto others — not respecting their boundaries or consent, you are in a far better position because one mustn’t justify wrong in the name of good and excuse it with the words, “I’m different” or “but this is different”. There is that which comes from within that is good, and that which comes from within that is bad. There is no doubt however, that an innocence existed in us all that was eventually corrupted and continues to be, keeping us drifted from our core, our anchor. Depending on when you discover this letter, it is my prayer that your innocence returns with no shadows to stifle it because the core has been made known to you, and you are anchored in the name of Jesus! This is merely a tool to wake you up and keep you active. That said, I trust that finding this and reading it to this point is a good sign of you being well on your way to unlocking hidden treasures within and about yourself, and the world around you. God bless you.
Truly,
MK.
