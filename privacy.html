WordSpin™

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who we are

WordSpin™ is a free word-prompt storytelling game available at wordspin.org. We are committed to being transparent and honest about how this site works and what happens with your data.

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use WordSpin, how it is used, and what choices you have.

2. What data we collect

WordSpin does not collect any personal information. Stories you write are saved only on your own device using your browser’s local storage. They are never uploaded to any server, never seen by us, and never shared with anyone. If you clear your browser data, your stories will be deleted from that device.

Your stories belong to you. They never leave your device.

Data collected automatically: When you visit WordSpin, standard technical information may be logged by web servers — including your approximate location (country or region), browser type, and device type. We do not use this information to identify you personally.

3. What are cookies and how do we use them

Cookies are small text files that a website saves onto your device when you visit. WordSpin currently uses only one type of cookie — preference cookies — to remember your chosen settings.

Preference Cookies

Used to remember your chosen settings — light or dark mode, sound on or off. These are stored locally on your device and never shared with anyone.

Essential Cookies

Required for basic site functionality such as saving your stories locally. Without these, the site will not work correctly. These cannot be switched off.

WordSpin does not currently use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or any third-party tracking. This policy will be updated if that changes.

4. Accounts and cloud storage

WordSpin does not currently offer user accounts. All stories are saved locally on your device only. An optional account system is planned for a future update — this policy will be updated in full before that feature is made available, and you will be asked to consent at that time.

5. Children and users under 13

WordSpin is designed to be suitable for all ages and we actively encourage its use as an educational tool for children.

Because WordSpin does not collect any personal data, it is safe for children of all ages to use. No account is required, no email address is collected, and no information leaves the device.

Parents and teachers can introduce WordSpin to young children with complete confidence that no personal information is captured or stored anywhere.

6. Your rights

Because WordSpin does not collect or store any personal data, there is no data held about you on our end. Your stories exist only on your own device and are entirely under your control.

You can delete your stories at any time using the delete function within the game, or by clearing your browser’s local storage in your device settings.

If you have any questions about your data rights, you are welcome to contact us at any time.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the site grows or new features are added. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date at the top. We encourage you to check back occasionally.

If significant changes are made — such as the introduction of advertising, accounts, or third-party data sharing — we will make this clearly visible on the site before those features go live.

8. Contact

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 5 business days.
